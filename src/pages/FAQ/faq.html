FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What Is Thrive?

Thrive is a platform that connects individuals seeking mental health support with licensed and certified therapists

How Does Thrive Work?

Users can sign up for an account on our website or mobile app and browse through our list of therapists to find one that meets your needs. Your can then book a session with the therapist.

Are There Qualified Therapist?

Yes, all therapists on thrive are licensed and certified mental health professionals.

Is My Information Confidential?

Yes, we take privacy and confidentiality very seriously. All sessions are encrypted and your information is protected.

How Long Does A session Last?

The duration of a standard session is fifty minutes to an hour.
